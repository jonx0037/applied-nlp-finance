# Chapter 2 — Building Your Financial Text Dataset: Advanced Exercises

## Exercise 2.6: Historical Dataset Construction
Build a research-quality dataset of all FOMC meeting minutes from 2000 to present with aligned market data (S&P 500, Treasury yields, VIX). Document your collection methodology, handling of edge cases, and data validation steps.

## Exercise 2.7: Alternative Data Evaluation
Identify a novel alternative text data source not covered in the chapter (e.g., patent filings, job postings, satellite imagery reports). Assess its potential alpha content, coverage, latency, and cost. Build a prototype scraper.
