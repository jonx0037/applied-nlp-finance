# Chapter 13 — Risk, Ethics, and Regulation: Advanced Exercises

## Exercise 13.6: Systemic Risk Assessment
Design a framework for assessing whether widespread adoption of similar NLP-based trading strategies could create systemic risk through herding behavior. Model a scenario where multiple funds react to the same text signal simultaneously and estimate the market impact.

## Exercise 13.7: Responsible AI Policy
Draft a responsible AI policy document for a hypothetical quantitative fund that uses NLP signals. Cover model governance, data provenance, bias monitoring, human oversight requirements, and incident response procedures. Align with existing industry frameworks (e.g., NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act).
