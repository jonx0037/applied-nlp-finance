# Chapter 13 — Risk, Ethics, and Regulation: Foundations Exercises

## Exercise 13.1: Failure Mode Analysis
Catalog at least five failure modes for a sentiment-based trading system (e.g., sarcasm misclassification, data source outage, adversarial text injection). For each, describe the mechanism, potential financial impact, and a mitigation strategy.

## Exercise 13.2: Regulatory Landscape Review
Survey the current regulatory landscape relevant to NLP-based trading systems across two jurisdictions (e.g., SEC/CFTC in the US and FCA in the UK). Identify specific regulations that may apply to text-based signal generation and automated trading.

## Exercise 13.3: Bias Audit
Conduct a bias audit on a financial sentiment model by testing its predictions across different entity categories (large-cap vs. small-cap, domestic vs. international, different industries). Report any systematic biases found.
