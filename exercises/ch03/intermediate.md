## Exercise 3.4: Domain Vocabulary Analysis
Compare the top-500 TF-IDF terms from a financial corpus against a general news corpus. Quantify the vocabulary divergence and identify terms that are uniquely important in finance.

## Exercise 3.5: Embedding Fine-Tuning
