# Chapter 7 — Central Bank Communications: Intermediate Exercises

## Exercise 7.4: Multi-Central-Bank Comparison
Extend the hawk/dove analysis framework to cover statements from at least two additional central banks (e.g., ECB, Bank of England). Adapt the keyword dictionaries for each institution and compare communication styles.

## Exercise 7.5: Rate Decision Nowcasting
Build a simple nowcasting model that predicts the next FOMC rate decision (hike, hold, cut) using only text features from the most recent statement and minutes. Compare performance against fed funds futures implied probabilities.
