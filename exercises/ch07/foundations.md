# Chapter 7 — Central Bank Communications: Foundations Exercises

## Exercise 7.1: FOMC Statement Parser
Build a parser that extracts and structures FOMC meeting statements, separating the policy decision, economic assessment, and forward guidance sections. Handle the varying formats used across different time periods.

## Exercise 7.2: Hawk/Dove Scoring
Implement a dictionary-based hawk/dove scoring system for FOMC statements using the keyword lists from the chapter. Score all statements from the past two years and plot the index over time alongside the federal funds rate.

## Exercise 7.3: Statement Diff Analysis
Implement a word-level diff between consecutive FOMC statements. Highlight additions, deletions, and modifications, then classify the net change direction as hawkish, dovish, or neutral.
