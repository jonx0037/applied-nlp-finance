# Chapter 7 — Central Bank Communications: Advanced Exercises

## Exercise 7.6: Forward Guidance Extraction
Build an LLM-based system that extracts structured forward guidance information from FOMC statements and press conferences: (a) the policy direction signal, (b) conditionality clauses, and (c) time horizon references. Evaluate extraction accuracy against manually labeled examples.

## Exercise 7.7: Communication Regime Changes
Analyze how FOMC communication style has evolved across different Fed chairs and policy regimes. Quantify changes in vocabulary complexity, statement length, hedging language, and information content. Relate these shifts to market reaction patterns.
