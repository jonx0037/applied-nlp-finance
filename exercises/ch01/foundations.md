# Chapter 1 — Why Text Moves Markets: Foundations Exercises

## Exercise 1.1: Financial Text Data Taxonomy
Classify 10 financial text sources from your own research into the structured/unstructured and scheduled/unscheduled taxonomy introduced in the chapter.

## Exercise 1.2: Historical Case Study Analysis
Select one of the historical examples mentioned in the chapter (Fed minutes, earnings calls, or social media). Find the original text and corresponding market data. Describe the relationship qualitatively.

## Exercise 1.3: Information Content Comparison
Using a single market event, compare the information content of (a) the price/volume data and (b) the associated text data (news article, press release, or filing). What does each tell you that the other does not?
