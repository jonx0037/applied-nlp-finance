# Chapter 9 — Entity Extraction and Knowledge Graphs: Intermediate Exercises

## Exercise 9.4: Entity Disambiguation
Implement an entity disambiguation module that resolves ambiguous company mentions (e.g., "Apple" the company vs. the fruit, "Amazon" the company vs. the river) using contextual clues from surrounding text.

## Exercise 9.5: Temporal Knowledge Graph
Extend your knowledge graph to track relationships over time. Implement point-in-time queries that return the graph state as of any historical date, and detect when relationships form or dissolve.
