# Chapter 4 — Transformers and LLMs for Financial Text: Advanced Exercises

## Exercise 4.6: LLM-as-Judge Labeling Pipeline
Build a complete LLM-as-judge pipeline for generating training labels at scale. Implement consensus mechanisms (majority vote across multiple prompts or models), measure inter-annotator agreement, and estimate label quality against a human-annotated gold set.

## Exercise 4.7: Model Distillation
Distill a large LLM's financial classification capabilities into a smaller, faster model suitable for production deployment. Measure the accuracy-latency-cost tradeoff across at least three model sizes.
