# Chapter 5 — From Text to Trading Signal: Intermediate Exercises

## Exercise 5.4: Multi-Signal Combination
Combine at least three NLP-derived signals (e.g., news sentiment, filing tone, social media buzz) into a single composite signal using both equal weighting and optimized weighting. Compare Sharpe ratios of each approach.

## Exercise 5.5: Signal Decay Analysis
Measure the predictive decay of a sentiment signal across horizons from 1 to 20 trading days. Plot the information coefficient at each horizon and identify the signal's effective half-life.
