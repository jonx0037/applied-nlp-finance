# Chapter 5 — From Text to Trading Signal: Advanced Exercises

## Exercise 5.6: Adaptive Signal Construction
Design a signal construction method that automatically adjusts its aggregation window and normalization parameters based on detected market regime. Implement and backtest the adaptive approach against fixed-parameter baselines.

## Exercise 5.7: Cross-Sectional Signal
Build a cross-sectional sentiment signal that ranks stocks within a universe by relative sentiment. Construct a long-short portfolio and evaluate its performance, turnover, and capacity constraints.
