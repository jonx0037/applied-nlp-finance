# Chapter 10 — Sentiment Regimes and Market States: Foundations Exercises

## Exercise 10.1: Hidden Markov Model Regime Detector
Implement a two-state Hidden Markov Model that classifies market regimes using sentiment score time series as the observed variable. Fit the model on historical data and visualize the inferred regime probabilities.

## Exercise 10.2: Regime Evaluation
Evaluate your regime detector by comparing inferred regimes against known market events (e.g., COVID crash, 2022 rate hikes). Compute regime persistence, average duration, and transition frequencies.

## Exercise 10.3: Regime-Conditional Statistics
Compute summary statistics (mean return, volatility, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown) for each detected regime. Test whether the differences across regimes are statistically significant.
