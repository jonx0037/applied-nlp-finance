# Chapter 6 — Earnings Calls and Corporate Filings: Foundations Exercises

## Exercise 6.1: Transcript Parser
Build a parser that splits an earnings call transcript into its structural components: operator remarks, prepared remarks (by speaker), and Q&A (by speaker). Output a structured DataFrame with speaker, section, and text columns.

## Exercise 6.2: Management Tone Analysis
Compute sentence-level sentiment scores for the prepared remarks section of five earnings call transcripts. Aggregate by call and visualize how management tone evolves across consecutive quarters for a single company.

## Exercise 6.3: Earnings Surprise Event Study
Construct a simple event study around earnings announcements. Compute cumulative abnormal returns in a [-1, +3] day window and correlate them with the sentiment of the corresponding earnings call.
