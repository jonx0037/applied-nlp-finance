# Chapter 11 — Backtesting NLP Strategies: Intermediate Exercises

## Exercise 11.4: Purged Cross-Validation
Implement purged k-fold cross-validation with an embargo period for time series data. Demonstrate that standard k-fold CV produces inflated performance estimates compared to the purged version on the same NLP signal.

## Exercise 11.5: Transaction Cost Modeling
Add realistic transaction cost modeling to your backtesting framework, including spread costs, market impact estimates, and borrowing costs for short positions. Measure how costs affect the net performance of a high-turnover NLP signal.
